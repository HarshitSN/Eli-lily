# Banking Voice Agent

An AI-powered voice calling system that automates debt collection and payment reminder calls using Vapi AI technology. This solution helps financial institutions reduce manual collection efforts while maintaining compliance and customer experience.

## 🎯 Key Features

### Voice Automation
- **Natural Conversations**: Human-like voice interactions using advanced AI
- **Personalized Scripts**: Context-aware messaging based on customer data
- **Multi-language Support**: Configurable for different languages and accents
- **Real-time Adaptation**: Dynamic conversation flow based on customer responses

### Debt Collection Management
- **Automated Outreach**: Systematic calling of customers with outstanding debts
- **Urgency-based Messaging**: Tailored communication based on debt age and amount
- **Payment Commitment Tracking**: Records and validates customer payment promises
- **Follow-up Scheduling**: Automated scheduling of subsequent calls

### Compliance & Analytics
- **Call Recording**: Full recording for compliance and quality assurance
- **Transcription Services**: Automatic speech-to-text for analysis
- **Success Metrics**: Detailed analytics on call outcomes and payment rates
- **Audit Trails**: Complete logs for regulatory compliance
