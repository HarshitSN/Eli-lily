## Overview

### The Challenge
*   Flight crews often deal with fragmented information across multiple legacy systems.
*   Weather disruptions cause cascading delays that are hard to predict manually.
*   Compliance regulations require strict adherence to crew rest times and certifications.

### Our Approach
*   **Unified Data Ingestion**: Examples ingestion of data from flight tracking, crew scheduling, and maintenance logs.
*   **Predictive AI Models**: Uses historical data to predict delay impacts and suggest optimal recovery plans.
*   **Natural Language Interface**: Crews and ops managers can ask complex questions like "Find a replacement captain for Flight 303 who is legal to fly to London."

### The Outcome
*   **30% Reduction** in crew scheduling conflicts during irregular operations.
*   **15% Improvement** in on-time performance for participating airlines.
*   **Faster Decision Making**: Reducing recovery planning time from hours to minutes.

## What's Included

*   **Crew Management Module**: Real-time tracking of crew legality and availability.
*   **Disruption Recovery**: AI-suggested rebooking and crew swapping scenarios.
*   **Maintenance Connector**: predictive alerts for aircraft maintenance needs.

## Integrations

*   **Jeppesen**: Direct integration for flight planning and charts.
*   **Sabre**: Sync with passenger service systems.
*   **ServiceNow**: Automated ticket creation for maintenance issues.

## FAQs
