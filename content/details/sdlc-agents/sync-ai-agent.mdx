# Sync AI - Roadmap & Risk Intelligence

Sync AI combines planning and risk intelligence to turn product strategy into execution-ready plans.

## What It Does

- Converts strategy inputs into a clear roadmap with milestones and delivery constraints
- Surfaces dependency and timeline risks early
- Produces mitigation guidance and practical execution notes
- Helps product and engineering teams align on plan realism

## Best For

- Product roadmap planning
- Cross-team dependency analysis
- Risk-aware release planning
- Program-level execution tracking
